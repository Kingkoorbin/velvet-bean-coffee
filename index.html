Crafted with Love, Served with Passion

Founded in 2015, Velvet Bean began as a dream shared between two coffee enthusiasts who believed that great coffee should be an experience, not just a drink. What started as a tiny espresso bar has grown into a community hub where friends gather.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, ensuring fair trade practices and the highest quality in every batch. Our master roasters bring out the unique character of each origin.

Our Philosophy

Brewed With Heart Since 2015.

Founded by two coffee obsessives in a 400-square-foot espresso bar, Velvet Bean was born from a singular belief: coffee is not a transactional caffeine delivery system—it is a daily sensory ritual.

We approach coffee like wine. We obsess over soil terroir, fermentation methods, and water chemistry to ensure that every shot pulled honors the labor of the farmers who grew it.
